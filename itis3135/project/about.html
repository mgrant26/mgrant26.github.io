About Me

My name is Emma, and I am a local Photographer based out of Charlotte, NC (but willing to travel!) I also am a student at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, with the expected graduation in 2024!

Growing up, I always had a passion for photography, but I never took it seriously and eventually lost my love. It wasn't until recently that I rediscovered my passion for it! I realized that it wasn't just the act of taking photos that brought me joy but also giving people unforgettable experiences that they can keep with them forever.

Helping people love themselves has always been a big passion of mine. Photography has been the key to spreading self-appreciation and happiness to more people! Not only does it help people love themselves in the moment, but it also gives them everlasting love in a photo; plus, who doesn't enjoy a glam moment?

When I'm not spreading love through pictures, I like to spread love to my 3-year-old puppy, Abbey, by taking her on walks and giving her shopping sprees. Between spoiling my puppy and being a student, I love playing my Wii and Switch and watching cringy movies with my boyfriend, Michael.

My goal and favorite thing to do is spread laughter/love and capture it, giving everyone an opportunity to be themselves!
